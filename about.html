About The Aeronauts

Our History, Our Mission, and Our Schools.

Our Mission

The La Crosse Robtics Team, or The Aeronauts, is a student-led team that incorporates students from all three of the La Crosse High Schools for a competition held by FIRST Robtics. Our goal is to build a robt that is best able to compete based on a set of rules given out in January.

There are many jobs on our robtics team, including building, modeling, coding, testing, driving, marketing, graphic design (posters, logos, buttons, stickers etc), costume design, promotion, scouting of other teams, advertising, fundraising, and more.

The Tri-School Alliance

We are unique because we unite students who are normally rivals in sports into one cohesive engineering unit.
