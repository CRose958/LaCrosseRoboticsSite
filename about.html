About The Aeronauts

Our History, Our Mission, and Our Schools.

Our Mission

Team 4054 "The Aeronauts" is more than just a robot-building club. We are a coalition of students from the School District of La Crosse dedicated to mastering STEM skills through the rigors of the FIRST Robotics Competition.

Our goal is to prepare students for the future workforce by operating like a technology startup. We have deadlines, budgets, marketing requirements, and engineering constraints.

The Tri-School Alliance

We are unique because we unite students who are normally rivals in sports into one cohesive engineering unit.

Central High School

Located in the heart of the city, bringing a legacy of academic excellence.

Logan High School

From the North Side, bringing strong community spirit and innovation.

Polytechnic
