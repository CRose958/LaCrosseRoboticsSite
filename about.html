About The Aeronauts

What We Do

The La Crosse Robotics Team, or The Aeronauts, is a student-led team that incorporates students from all three of the La Crosse High Schools for a competition held by FIRST Robotics. Our goal is to build a robot that is best able to compete based on a set of rules given out in January.

There are many jobs on our robotics team, including building, modeling, coding, testing, driving, marketing, graphic design (posters, logos, buttons, stickers etc), costume design, promotion, scouting of other teams, advertising, fundraising, and more.

The District-Wide Alliance

Other teams are usually comprised of one or two schools, but our team has three schools, each giving us unique experiences and skills.

Central High School

Located in the heart of the city, bringing a legacy of academic excellence.

Logan High School

From the North Side, bringing strong community spirit and innovation.

Polytechnic

Our project-based learning center, providing specialized hands-on expertise.
