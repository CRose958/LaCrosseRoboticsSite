Mission

We Operate Like a Startup

This is not just a club. We unite students to operate like a small technology company.

Engineering

Designing and fabricating industrial-sized robots capable of competing in high-intensity arenas.

Computer Science

Developing code for autonomous operation and driver control systems using industry-standard languages.

Business

Managing budgets, marketing the team brand, and securing sponsorship to sustain operations.

A Regional STEM Hub

Beyond the local team, La Crosse Robotics serves as a pillar for STEM in the region.

We host the major Seven Rivers Regional tournament at the La Crosse Center and foster the Seven Rivers Robotics Coalition to support cooperative competition across the tri-state area.
