Who We Are

La Crosse Robotics is a high school engineering community centered on the School District of La Crosse's FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team, Team 4054 "The Aeronauts."

Our members design, build, and program robots to solve complex annual challenges for the Competitions.

A Regional STEM Hub

Beyond the local team, La Crosse Robotics serves as a pillar for STEM in the region.

We participate in the major Seven Rivers Regional tournament at the La Crosse Center and foster the Seven Rivers Robotics Coalition to support cooperative competition across the tri-state area.
