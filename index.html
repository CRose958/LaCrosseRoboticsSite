Engineering

Designing and fabricating industrial-sized robots capable of competing in high-intensity arenas.

A Regional STEM Hub

Beyond the local team, La Crosse Robotics serves as a pillar for STEM in the region.

We participate the major Seven Rivers Regional tournament at the La Crosse Center and foster the Seven Rivers Robotics Coalition to support cooperative competition across the tri-state area.
